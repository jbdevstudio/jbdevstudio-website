This folder contains composite sites which allow a user to resolve all upstream dependencies of a Red Hat JBoss Developer Studio JBoss Central connector via a single URL. Simply launch JBoss Developer Studio, open JBoss Central, then select the Software/Update tab to explore this site for installable connectors.

Content available from JBoss Central has been tested by Red Hat but support of the content varies depending on content type:


  • Supported
    These plug-ins are hosted by Red Hat, tested by Red Hat to ensure compatibility with JBoss Developer Studio, and supported by Red Hat.
  • Tested
    These plug-ins are hosted by Red Hat and tested by Red Hat, but support is most likely provided by another vendor. Red Hat attempts to report issues to providers as required, should any arise.
  • Technology Preview
    These plug-ins are tested by Red Hat, often with documentation describing known issues. The expectation is that these technology preview plug-ins may one day evolve sufficiently to be considered Tested or Supported, but this is not guaranteed.
  • Experimental
    These are experimental plug-ins. It is possible that their content may change at any time and without prior notice. Red Hat attempts to monitor this content and execute appropriate tests, reporting issues to plug-in providers as required. Experimental content may evolve into Technology Preview content, but this is not guaranteed.

Most content in JBoss Central is hosted (redistributed) by Red Hat; however, some connectors cannot be redistributed due to license terms or other constraints, and therefore have been marked accordingly:


  • External
    These plug-ins are tested by Red Hat but, as the plug-ins are hosted by external third party providers, it is possible that content may change at any time and without prior notice. Red Hat attempts to monitor the associated third party sites and execute appropriate tests on plug-ins, reporting issues to plug-in providers as required.
